# Power BI Supply Chain Analysis Project Overview:
This Power BI project analyzes sales and supply chain data across plant locations, providing insights on revenue, product performance, and customer segments.
It highlights regional trends, identifies top-performing products, and tracks operational efficiency metrics such as inventory levels and delivery performance and 
interactive dashboards enable stakeholders to make data-driven decisions and optimize overall supply chain operations.

# Key Insights & Features:
1.Revenue & Profit: Total revenue $577.6K, total profit $524.68K.
2.Plant Performance: Revenue and quantity sold by plant location.
3.Vendor Lead Times: Delivery times by vendor with order quantities.
4.Shipping Trends: Monthly shipping time trends.
5.Revenue Share: By plant location and product category (Hammer, Drill, Nuts & Bolts).
6.Delivery Analysis: Delivery delays vs. work order duration.
7.Interactive Filters: Business Unit, Plant Location, SKU.
